Vegan Restraunt

a London City eatery

CAULIFLOWER SHAWARMA $20
Vegetable dishes don’t come much more thrilling than this smoky, richly-spiced whole cauliflower charred on the grill and adorned with tahini, pomegranate seeds and pine nuts. A match for any meat dish.

CAULIFLOWER GYOZA $15
The cauliflower gyoza (actually more of a cauliflower tortellini) turned out to be a real winner, despite its vegetarian credentials.

ICE CREAM SANDWICH $25
This delicious chicken shop doesn't skimp on dessert. Bird's Doughnut Hatch offers delicious sweet menu, including this to-die-for ice cream sandwich.
